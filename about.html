Who We Are

Personally Fit, RSF is a personal training studio located in Fairbanks Ranch Plaza (Rancho Santa Fe area) and has been in operation there since 1993. We provide quality fitness programs, functional training and rehabilitative exercise to people of all ages and all levels of ability. Our staff of highly educated trainers work with each client to define their strengths, weaknesses and personal goals and direct them on the path to success with their overall health and fitness.


At Personally Fit, we provide a comfortable atmosphere where even the non-athlete can feel at home.
